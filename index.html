HOPE REVIVAL CENTER

About

In as much as their are more than 1.5 million Kenyans living with HIV&AIDS,still their are many who don't know the repuctions of the disease and they don't know what to do when they get incontact with the disease.We have seen many of them suffer in silence leading to their deaths.It is indeed a high time to raise our voices even higher and stop the tendency of loosing our beloved ones and yet they could have lived for as long as they would like with the use of ARVs and even healthier lives.Lets stand with the community as a whole and make it open to everyone that getting infected with HIV&AIDS is not the end of life but instead it is a beggining of a new journey.Not any other journey but a journey which needs committment.Lets stand together and revive our society.Choose to live and choose to go through it.With Hope Revival Center, together we will overcome.

Main Objectives

  1. To collaborate with stakeholders,and other Non-governmental Organisations(NGo's) to address ,prevent and provide control measures to curb the spread of HIV&AIDS.
  2. To handle and address the issues of stigma and behaviour change within the society of the infected persons.
  3. To work together with the government and the ministry of Education to introduce the HIV&AIDS education in schools for the deaf and the dumb.

Services

  • To engage the support member groups in an income generating activities.
  • To educate them(members)on the importance of proper nutrition and other related diseases.
  • To highlight the importance of taking ARV's drugs as prescribed.
  • To Educate on risks of defaulting and help in defaulter training and follow ups.
  • To make referrals to hospital facilities.
  • To arrange for testing and councelling at the community level in conjunction with the ministry of Health.
